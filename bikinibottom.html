About Bikini Bottom

Bikini Bottom is the vibrant underwater city where SpongeBob SquarePants and his friends live.

Top Attractions

  • - Krusty Krab: Enjoy delicious Krabby Patties!
  • - Jellyfish Fields: Go jellyfishing with SpongeBob.
  • - Goo Lagoon: A beach for fun and sunbathing.
  • - Sandy's Treehouse: Experience Texas-style hospitality underwater.

Travel Tips

  • - Bring your net for jellyfishing.
  • - Be careful at Jellyfish Fields.
  • - Stay away from Chum Bucket!

About the Author

SpongeBob SquarePants is a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and a proud resident of Bikini Bottom. He loves jellyfishing, bubble blowing, and sharing his adventures with the world.
